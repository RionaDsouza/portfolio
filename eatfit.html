EatFit

EatFit is an app that aims to help young adults cook and eat healthier food.

Project Overview

EatFit is an app for healthy recipes. Using this app young adults can cook healthier versions of what they want to eat.
I designed this app as part of the Google certificate. I have tried to follow the complete design process while deisgning the app.
The project includes a dedicated mobile app, a desktop website and a mobile-only website.


Project Duration

It took me 2 weeks to complete the project.


My Roles

sole UX designer, UX researcher


My Responsibilities

research, ideation, wireframing, prototyping, responsive design, testing, iterating on designs


User Research Summary

I conducted a survey to collect views of people I knew. Most of the responses I got suggested that they didn't really care about eating healthy. The main cause of this lack of resources. Most people thought that eating healthy meant that they would have to give up on tasty food. However, with the right recipe and minimum efforts, they wouldn't mind trying out some new recipes.

User Personas

Ideation

For the ideation, I took the traditional approch of using pen and paper. I noted down all the features that the app would have. This gave me a clear direction of what I wanted the app to be.

Paper Wireframes

Once i had a vision for the app, I drew some paper wireframes. These designs mainly focused on the best layouts for screens.

Digital Wireframes

After the paper wireframes, I moved on to create the digital wireframes. For this project I used Figma for designing.

Low-Fidelity Prototype

I connected the screens to create the main user flow. Based on this prototype, I conducted a usability study to get feedback about the app.


Prototype Link


Usability Study Findings

Iconography

Due to lack to proper icons, users did not understand what actions to perform.

Flexibility

Users wanted an option to switch between veg and non-veg variations of the recipe.

Confirmation

Once the items were added to the list, users wanted a confirmation to know whether their task was completed.

Mockups

Based on the feedback I received from the usability study, I refined my designs and made the required changes.

High-Fidelity Prototype

The high fidelity prototype followed the same flow as the low fidelity prototype. I added some more screens in the flow to make the experience better.

Responsive Design

Along with the app, I designed a responsive website for the app. The website had similar features as the app. For the website, I designed for the mobile-view and the desktop view.

Accessibility Considerations

• All the text follows clear hierarchy for easy reading and understanding.
• Clear iconography that allows for easy navigation.


Impact

EatFit aims to build a community of young adults who enjoy cooking as much as they enjoy eating. The app aims to build healthy habits among the youth by providing easy alternatives for their favourite recipes.


What I learned

Through this project I learned to listen to the users and their needs. User feedback is very essential in getting optimum results.
