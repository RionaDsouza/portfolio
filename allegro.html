Allegro

Allegro is a musician booking app to make all event planning easier.

Project Overview

This was the first project that I completed as part of the Google certificate. I had to work on an app as per a design prompt given to me.


The Problem
In the present day, planning an event is no easy task. For individuals with full-time jobs, it is all the more tough. This app aims to make the process simpler.

The Goal
Allegro will help users easily book musicians for their events from the comfort of their home. Users can see the kind of musicians they would want. Work done by the musicians previously will be shown so the users can get a better understanding of the musicians' work.

My Roles
sole UX designer, UX researcher

My Responsibilities
research, ideation, wireframing, prototyping, responsive design, testing, iterating on designs

User Research Summary

I conducted interviews to understand the users better. Primary users were working individuals who don't have a lot of time to spend in planning events/functions.

Initially, the app focused on booking musicians to play at weddings. But after conducting a competitive audit, I decided to expand the idea for all kinds of events.

User Pain Points

• planning an event while holding a full-time job
• not being able to view the previous work of the musicians
• not getting enough options to select from

Impact

Allegro helps users to book musicians from the ease of their home.
By allowing the users to view videos and customer reviews, Allegro gives users a better experience.


What I learned

While designing the app, I realised that research plays an important role in the process.
While the first design is important, it is more essential to keep iterating on the designs.
